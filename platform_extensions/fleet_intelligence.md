# Case Study: Fleet Intelligence Platform

## Business Problem

Fleet operators manage thousands of connected vehicles generating continuous telemetry, safety events, and operational data. Traditional monitoring systems are reactive and provide limited decision support.

## Platform Signals

- GPS Location
- Vehicle Telemetry
- Driver Behavior
- Fuel Consumption
- Engine Diagnostics
- Maintenance Events
- Route Performance
- Weather Conditions

## Decision Engine

The platform continuously evaluates:

- Driver Risk
- Fleet Health
- Maintenance Priority
- Route Optimization
- Safety Alerts

## Business Outcomes

- Improved driver safety
- Reduced fuel costs
- Predictive maintenance
- Higher fleet utilization
- Lower operational risk

## Shared Platform Components

The Fleet Intelligence Platform reuses:

- Event Streaming
- Edge Processing
- Risk Engine
- Policy Engine
- Decision Engine
- Analytics Platform

Only the domain-specific signals differ.
