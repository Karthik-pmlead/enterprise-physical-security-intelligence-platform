# Case Study: Industrial IoT

## Business Problem

Manufacturing environments require continuous monitoring of machines, production lines, and industrial assets to minimize downtime and maintain operational efficiency.

## Platform Signals

- Machine Telemetry
- Equipment Health
- Production Metrics
- Sensor Data
- Maintenance Records
- Environmental Conditions

## Platform Decisions

- Predict failures
- Schedule maintenance
- Detect anomalies
- Optimize production

## Business Outcomes

- Reduced downtime
- Improved equipment utilization
- Better operational efficiency
