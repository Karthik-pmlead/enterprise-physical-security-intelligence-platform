# Case Study: Smart Buildings

## Business Problem

Modern buildings generate operational data across HVAC, lighting, occupancy, access, and energy systems.

## Platform Signals

- Occupancy Sensors
- HVAC Systems
- Energy Consumption
- Access Events
- Environmental Sensors

## Platform Decisions

- Optimize energy usage
- Detect anomalies
- Predict maintenance
- Improve occupant comfort
- Trigger operational alerts

## Business Outcomes

- Lower operating costs
- Increased sustainability
- Improved facility management
